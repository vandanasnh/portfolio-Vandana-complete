  • The Challenge : Detecting brain tumors early is critical for successful treatment. But traditional methods can be time-consuming and prone to human error.

  • Our Weapon : A powerful AI model built with TensorFlow & Keras. It analyzes MRI images to identify brain tumors with 88.7% accuracy.

  • The Advantage : Faster, more accurate diagnoses that could save lives.

How We Did It:

  • Data Warriors : We used data augmentation to squeeze more training data from existing images, making our AI model stronger.
